Projects

1. Course Finder Allocation

Course Finder Allocation is a system designed to streamline the process of course selection and enrollment for students. It involves creating a student profile that includes personal information, academic history, and course preferences. Students browse a course catalog, which provides details such as course descriptions, schedules, prerequisites, and capacities. They then select and rank their preferred courses before submitting their choices within a specified timeframe.

2. Digital Craft Studio

Digital Craft Studio is a platform designed to enhance the creative process for artists and makers by integrating traditional crafting techniques with modern technology. It offers a versatile digital workspace with tools for drawing, painting, sculpting, and 3D modeling, along with a resource library filled with templates, patterns, tutorials, and material simulations. Collaboration tools enable users to work on shared projects, receive feedback, and engage in community discussions.
